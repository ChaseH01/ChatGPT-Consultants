our story

Founder Chase Hurwitz is a candidate for his B.S. in computer science from Washington University in St. Louis, a top 15 school in America. He started automAIte after his parents begged him to teach them how to use ChatGPT. He quickly realized that there was a significant demand for personalized guidance on utilizing ChatGPT's potential to improve lives and businesses. Specifically, non-digital natives lacked the technical fluency to feel confident in using ChatGPT. Driven by a passion for democratizing AI knowledge, Chase founded automAIte with a clear mission in mind: to empower individuals and small businesses with the transformative capabilities of ChatGPT.

At automAIte, our primary goal is to make the power of AI accessible to everyone. We believe that ChatGPT has the potential to revolutionize personal productivity, foster creativity, and enhance problem-solving for businesses. Our mission is to bridge the gap between advanced AI technology and everyday users by providing expert guidance and tailored solutions.

Our services cater to diverse needs, from individual Q/A sessions, ensuring each customer receives the attention and answers they deserve, to insightful personal info sessions that equip individuals with a comprehensive understanding of AI and ChatGPT. Additionally, we offer corporate consultations, enabling businesses to harness AI's advantages to optimize their workflows, improve decision-making processes, and drive growth.

Join us on this journey of AI exploration, and let automAIte be your trusted partner in harnessing the power of ChatGPT to unlock new possibilities and transform the way you approach challenges. Together, we envision a future where AI-driven solutions are within reach for everyone, empowering individuals and businesses alike to thrive in an ever-evolving digital landscape.
